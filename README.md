# Z-Combinator

**The Adversarial Startup Bootcamp**

Z-Combinator takes founders from raw idea through validation, building, launching, and scaling — with hard quality gates, founder profiling, and kill points at every stage.

This isn't a gentle incubator. It's a pressure cooker. Ideas can and will be killed. Founders will be challenged at every step. The goal is to fail fast, fail cheap, and fail with maximum learning.

## How It Works

Say `/z-combinator` or "start the bootcamp" to begin.

### The Pipeline

| Stage | Name | Question | Gate |
|-------|------|----------|------|
| 0 | Onboarding | Who are you? | Profile complete |
| 1 | Crucible | Is this worth anyone's time? | 60/100 (Kill Point) |
| 2 | Validation | Will anyone pay for this? | 70/100 (Kill Point) |
| 3 | Scope | What's the smallest thing that proves the thesis? | 70/100 |
| 4 | Architecture | How do we build it right? | 75/100 |
| 5 | Operations | How do you run a company? | 65/100 |
| 6 | Build | Execute the plan | 80/100 (Kill Point) |
| 7 | Quality | Is this ready for real humans? | 80/100 |
| 8 | Launch | Put it in front of the world | 70/100 |
| 9 | Measure | Is it working? | 60/100 (Pivot Point) |
| 10 | Scale | What got you here won't get you there | Continuous |

### Kill Points

Gates 1, 2, and 6 can recommend you **stop entirely**. Gate 9 can recommend a **pivot**. Every gate has hard-fail triggers that cause automatic failure regardless of score.

### Founder Profiling

Before anything else, Z-Combinator interviews you to understand your builder archetype, strengths, gaps, risk profile, and working style. This profile customizes the entire bootcamp — pushing harder where you're weak, providing more support where you need it.
